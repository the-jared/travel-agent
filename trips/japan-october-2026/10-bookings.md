# Bookings & Action Items — Japan October 2026

> All booking links and action items organized by urgency. Dates assume a mid-to-late October 2026 trip.

---

## Book Now (6+ Months Before)

These sell out or prices jump significantly. Do not delay.

### Flights
- [ ] **Round-trip flights to Tokyo (NRT/HND)**
  - Search: [Google Flights](https://www.google.com/travel/flights) | [Skyscanner](https://www.skyscanner.com) | [Momondo](https://www.momondo.com)
  - Set price alerts on Google Flights for your dates
  - Consider: ANA/JAL direct if available from your hub; stopover options via Seoul (Korean Air) or Taipei (EVA Air)
  - **Tip:** Book by July 2026 at the latest. October is peak season (fall foliage).

### Kyoto Ryokan
- [ ] **Traditional ryokan for 1-2 nights in Kyoto**
  - [Booking.com ryokan search](https://www.booking.com/ryokan/city/jp/kyoto.html)
  - [Japanese Guest Houses](https://www.japaneseguesthouses.com/ryokan-search/?prefecture=Kyoto)
  - [Ikyu.com](https://www.ikyu.com) (Japanese booking site — better selection, use Google Translate)
  - Recommended areas: Higashiyama (walkable to temples), Arashiyama (scenic)
  - Budget: 25,000-60,000 JPY/night for a quality ryokan with kaiseki dinner
  - **Tip:** October is peak ryokan season. Book 6+ months ahead or you'll only find overpriced options.

### Kyoto Oct 22 Hotel
- [ ] **Hotel for remaining Kyoto nights (non-ryokan)**
  - [Booking.com](https://www.booking.com) | [Agoda](https://www.agoda.com) (often cheaper for Asia)
  - Recommended: Kawaramachi/Gion area for walkability
  - Budget: 10,000-20,000 JPY/night
  - **Tip:** Free cancellation rates are slightly higher but give you flexibility. Book now, optimize later.

### JR Pass Decision
- [ ] **Decide and purchase Japan Rail Pass (if using)**
  - [JR Pass Official](https://japanrailpass.net/)
  - 7-day Ordinary: ~50,000 JPY (2026 pricing TBC)
  - Worth it if doing Tokyo-Kyoto-Osaka round trip + day trips
  - Calculator: [JR Pass Calculator](https://www.jrpass.com/farecalculator)
  - **Tip:** Must be purchased before arrival. Can activate on any date after purchase.

## Book 2 Months Before (August 2026)

### TeamLab
- [ ] **TeamLab Borderless (Azabudai Hills, Tokyo)**
  - [tickets.teamlab.art](https://tickets.teamlab.art/)
  - Weekday tickets sell out 2-4 weeks ahead; weekends sell out 4-6 weeks ahead
  - Book a weekday morning slot (first entry) for smallest crowds
  - Cost: ~3,200 JPY
  - **Tip:** Wear solid dark clothing for best immersive photo effect. No heels (mirror floors).

### Michelin Restaurants
- [ ] **High-end dining reservations**
  - [Tabelog](https://tabelog.com) — Japan's definitive restaurant rating site
  - [Omakase](https://www.omakase.in) — English-language booking for high-end Tokyo restaurants
  - [TableAll](https://www.tableall.com) — Concierge service for hard-to-book restaurants
  - [Pocket Concierge](https://www.pocketconcierge.jp/en) — Book and pre-pay Michelin restaurants
  - Target restaurants (book early):
    - [ ] Sushi — mid-range omakase (Sushi Saito is impossible; try Sushi Shin or similar)
    - [ ] Tempura — Mikawa Zezankyo (Tokyo) or Yoshikawa (Kyoto)
    - [ ] Kaiseki — Kikunoi (Kyoto) or Nihonryori RyuGin (Tokyo)
  - **Tip:** Many top spots only accept reservations via phone in Japanese. Use your hotel concierge or TableAll.

### Cooking Classes
- [ ] **Japanese cooking class**
  - [airKitchen](https://airkitchen.me) — Home cooking with locals
  - [Hana Cooking Class](https://www.hanacookingclass.com) (Kyoto)
  - [Buddha Bellies](https://buddhabelliestokyo.com) (Tokyo)
  - Ramen, sushi-making, or bento class options
  - Cost: 5,000-10,000 JPY per person
  - **Tip:** Kyoto classes focused on washoku (traditional cuisine) are special. Book weekday mornings.

### Hakone
- [ ] **Hakone accommodation (if doing day trip / overnight)**
  - [Booking.com Hakone](https://www.booking.com/city/jp/hakone.html)
  - Consider an onsen ryokan for the full experience
  - **Tip:** October weather is ideal for Hakone. Clear days = Mt. Fuji views.

## Book 1 Week Before (Early-Mid October 2026)

### Walking Tours
- [ ] **Guided walking tours**
  - [Tokyo Localized](https://www.tokyolocalized.com) — Free walking tours (tip-based)
  - [Magical Trip](https://www.magicaltrip.com) — Food tours, bar hopping tours
  - [Inside Kyoto](https://www.insidekyoto.com) — Chris Rowthorn's private tours
  - Recommended: Tsukiji Outer Market food tour, Gion evening walk, Fushimi Inari early morning
  - **Tip:** Free tours fill up but not months ahead. One week is fine.

### Remaining Restaurants
- [ ] **Mid-range restaurant reservations**
  - Use Tabelog or Google Maps to find 3.5+ rated spots near your hotels
  - Izakaya, ramen, and casual spots generally don't need reservations
  - Reserve for: weekend dinners, groups of 3+, any restaurant with counter seating only
  - **Tip:** Many good restaurants are walk-in only. Having a backup plan is better than over-scheduling.

### Day Trip Logistics
- [ ] **Specific train times for day trips**
  - [Hyperdia](http://www.hyperdia.com) or [Jorudan](https://world.jorudan.co.jp/mln/en/) for route planning
  - Plan: Hakone day trip routing, Nara day trip from Kyoto, Fushimi Inari early morning timing

## Book on Arrival (In Japan)

### Transit Cards
- [ ] **Suica or Pasmo IC card**
  - Available at any JR station ticket machine (airport stations included)
  - Load 3,000-5,000 JPY to start
  - Works on: all Tokyo/Kyoto/Osaka trains, buses, convenience stores, vending machines
  - **Note:** Physical Suica cards have been limited. Check if Apple Wallet Suica is available for your phone, or get a Welcome Suica (28-day tourist version) at the airport.

### Hakone Free Pass
- [ ] **Hakone Free Pass (if doing Hakone day trip)**
  - Purchase at Odakyu Shinjuku Station
  - 2-day pass: ~6,100 JPY from Shinjuku
  - Includes: round-trip train, all Hakone buses, ropeway, pirate ship, cable car
  - **Tip:** Buy at the Odakyu Sightseeing Service Center (English support). Get the Romance Car upgrade (+1,110 JPY) for reserved seats with views.

### Pocket WiFi / SIM
- [ ] **Pocket WiFi or travel SIM**
  - Airport pickup: [Japan Wireless](https://www.japan-wireless.com) | [Ninja WiFi](https://ninjawifi.com)
  - eSIM option: [Ubigi](https://www.ubigi.com) | [Airalo](https://www.airalo.com) (activate before landing)
  - **Tip:** eSIM is cheapest and instant. Pocket WiFi is better for groups sharing one device.

### Cash
- [ ] **Withdraw JPY at airport ATM**
  - 7-Eleven and Japan Post ATMs accept international cards
  - Withdraw 30,000-50,000 JPY to start
  - **Tip:** Many small restaurants, temples, and shops are cash-only. Always carry at least 10,000 JPY.
