Money Tips

  • Cash is still important. Many small restaurants, shrines, and market stalls are cash-only. Carry ¥30,000-50,000 (~$200-350).
  • 7-Eleven ATMs accept international Visa/Mastercard. Withdraw in larger amounts to minimize fees.
  • Credit cards accepted at hotels, department stores, chain restaurants, and konbini. Visa/Mastercard preferred over Amex.
  • IC cards (Suica/ICOCA) can be charged at station machines or via Apple Pay. Use for transit and small purchases.
  • No tipping. Ever. It can be considered rude.
  • Tax-free shopping available at stores displaying "Tax Free" for purchases over ¥5,000. Bring your passport.
  • Exchange rate assumption: This guide uses approximately $1 = ¥145. Check current rates before departure.
