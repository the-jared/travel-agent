Cultural Quick Reference — The Big 3 Rules

  • **Shoes off** — Remove shoes when entering homes, ryokans, some restaurants, and temples. Look for a raised floor or shoe rack at the entrance. Slippers are usually provided.
  • **Quiet in public transit** — Trains and buses are silent zones. Phone calls are a no-go. Set your phone to silent (マナーモード). Speak softly if you must.
  • **No tipping, ever** — Tipping is not customary and can cause confusion or offense. The price on the menu is what you pay. Service is already exceptional.
