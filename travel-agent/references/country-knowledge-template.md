# Country Knowledge Template

## Research Framework

When researching a destination country/region, gather ALL of the following.
Mark any item that cannot be found with "⚠️ Not confirmed — verify independently."

---

### 1. Entry Requirements
- [ ] Visa requirements for US passport holders
- [ ] Visa requirements for EU passport holders
- [ ] Visa requirements for UK passport holders
- [ ] Visa requirements for Australian passport holders
- [ ] Visa-on-arrival availability and cost
- [ ] e-Visa availability and processing time
- [ ] Passport validity requirement (6 months? 3 months?)
- [ ] Blank passport pages requirement
- [ ] Return/onward ticket requirement
- [ ] Proof of accommodation requirement
- [ ] Proof of funds requirement
- [ ] COVID-19 entry requirements (if any remain)
- [ ] Customs declarations (cash limits, prohibited items)
- [ ] Departure tax (included in ticket or paid at airport?)

### 2. Health & Safety
- [ ] Required vaccinations
- [ ] Recommended vaccinations
- [ ] Malaria risk zones (if applicable)
- [ ] Dengue/Zika risk assessment
- [ ] Tap water safety
- [ ] Food safety general guidance
- [ ] Common traveler illnesses
- [ ] Pharmacy accessibility and common medications
- [ ] Quality of local healthcare
- [ ] Travel insurance requirements or recommendations
- [ ] Emergency numbers (police, ambulance, fire, tourist police)
- [ ] Nearest embassy/consulate locations
- [ ] Government travel advisory level (1-4 scale)
- [ ] Common scams targeting tourists (with specific examples)
- [ ] Areas to avoid (specific neighborhoods/regions)
- [ ] Natural disaster risks (earthquakes, typhoons, flooding season)
- [ ] Altitude sickness risk (if applicable)

### 3. Money & Costs
- [ ] Local currency name and symbol
- [ ] Current exchange rate to USD
- [ ] Current exchange rate to EUR
- [ ] Current exchange rate to GBP
- [ ] Best way to get local currency (ATM vs exchange vs card)
- [ ] ATM availability and fees
- [ ] Credit card acceptance level (widespread/limited/rare)
- [ ] Contactless payment availability
- [ ] Tipping customs (restaurants, taxis, hotels, tours)
- [ ] Bargaining/haggling customs (where acceptable, starting offers)
- [ ] Average daily budget: Budget tier ($X-Y)
- [ ] Average daily budget: Mid-range tier ($X-Y)
- [ ] Average daily budget: Luxury tier ($X-Y)
- [ ] VAT/tax refund for tourists (available? process?)
- [ ] Common tourist price gouging to watch for

### 4. Communication
- [ ] Official language(s)
- [ ] English proficiency level (widespread/moderate/limited/rare)
- [ ] Essential phrases (20 minimum):
  - Hello / Goodbye / Thank you / Please / Sorry / Excuse me
  - Yes / No / How much? / Where is...? / I don't understand
  - Help! / Water / Food / Bathroom / Check please
  - Numbers 1-10 / Do you speak English?
- [ ] Alphabet/script used (Latin, Cyrillic, Arabic, CJK, etc.)
- [ ] Google Translate offline language pack availability
- [ ] SIM card options (airport purchase? tourist SIM?)
- [ ] eSIM availability and recommended providers
- [ ] WiFi availability (cafes, hotels, public)
- [ ] VPN needed? (China, Russia, UAE, etc.)
- [ ] Blocked apps/services (WhatsApp, Google, Facebook, etc.)
- [ ] Best local messaging app (WeChat, LINE, KakaoTalk, etc.)

### 5. Culture & Etiquette
- [ ] Dress code norms (general, temples, business, beaches)
- [ ] Shoe removal customs (homes, temples, some restaurants)
- [ ] Greeting customs (bow, handshake, wai, kiss, etc.)
- [ ] Gift-giving etiquette
- [ ] Photography etiquette (people, temples, military, royalty)
- [ ] Religious sensitivities
- [ ] LGBTQ+ safety and legal status
- [ ] Solo female traveler safety notes
- [ ] Public displays of affection (acceptable?)
- [ ] Alcohol laws and customs
- [ ] Drug laws (severity — some countries have death penalty)
- [ ] Smoking laws
- [ ] Noise/behavior customs (quiet trains, loud markets)
- [ ] Business hours (shops, restaurants, attractions)
- [ ] Siesta/rest period (if applicable)
- [ ] Weekend days (Fri-Sat in some countries)
- [ ] National holidays during trip dates
- [ ] Political situation and protests (current)
- [ ] Royal/government sensitivities (Thailand lese-majeste, etc.)

### 6. Practical Infrastructure
- [ ] Power outlet type(s) and voltage
- [ ] Adapter recommendation
- [ ] Driving side (left/right)
- [ ] International driving permit needed?
- [ ] Road quality and driving culture assessment
- [ ] Public transport quality assessment
- [ ] Ride-hailing apps available (Uber, Grab, Bolt, etc.)
- [ ] Postal service reliability
- [ ] Laundry options (laundromats, hotel service)
- [ ] Luggage storage services
- [ ] Public toilet availability and customs (pay, BYO paper, squat)
- [ ] Water bottle refill culture (bring reusable?)
- [ ] Recycling/waste customs

### 7. Climate & Packing
- [ ] Climate classification
- [ ] Historical temperature range for trip dates
- [ ] Historical rainfall for trip dates
- [ ] Humidity levels
- [ ] UV index typical levels
- [ ] Rainy season dates
- [ ] Best months to visit
- [ ] Worst months to visit
- [ ] Typhoon/hurricane/cyclone season
- [ ] Snow season (if applicable)
- [ ] Sunrise/sunset times for trip dates
- [ ] Daylight hours

---

## Output Format

Present all findings in a structured guide with sections matching the above.
Use tables for comparisons, callout boxes for warnings, and checklists for
actionable items. Always note the source and recency of information.
