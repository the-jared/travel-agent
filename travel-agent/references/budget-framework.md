### Money-Saving Tips
For each destination, include:
- Best value activities (free walking tours, free museum days)
- Lunch deals / set menus that offer dinner quality at lunch prices
- Transit pass savings vs individual tickets
- Shoulder season pricing differences
- Booking timing advice (how far in advance for best prices)
- Cash vs. card cost differences
- Airport exchange vs. city ATM savings
- Duty-free opportunities worth pursuing
- Tax refund eligibility and process

### Splurge-Worthy Expenses
For each destination, identify 3-5 "worth the splurge" items:
- Experiences you can't get at home
- Once-in-a-lifetime activities
- Where the luxury tier delivers 10x the budget experience
- Where the budget tier is perfectly fine (don't waste money)
