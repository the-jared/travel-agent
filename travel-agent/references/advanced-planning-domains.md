# Advanced Planning Domains

## Domains Beyond Traditional Travel Agents

This reference expands the skill's coverage into emerging and specialized
travel planning areas that most agents don't touch.

---

## 1. Digital Nomad & Remote Work Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Coworking spaces (ratings, cost/day, WiFi speed, amenities)
- [ ] Cafe culture for working (power outlets, WiFi, acceptable stay duration)
- [ ] Digital nomad visa availability
- [ ] Time zone overlap with team/clients
- [ ] Internet reliability and speed (check Speedtest data, Nomad List)
- [ ] Cost of living comparison (Numbeo, Nomad List)
- [ ] Coliving spaces (if longer stay)
- [ ] Networking events / meetups for digital nomads
- [ ] Mail forwarding / virtual mailbox services

## 2. Sustainable & Responsible Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Eco-certified accommodations (Green Key, EarthCheck, LEED)
- [ ] Carbon offset options for flights
- [ ] Responsible wildlife tourism (avoid elephant riding, tiger temples)
- [ ] Ethical voluntourism opportunities
- [ ] Local economy impact (choose local businesses over chains)
- [ ] Plastic reduction (refill stations, bamboo straws, reusable bags)
- [ ] Overtourism awareness (visit off-peak, lesser-known alternatives)
- [ ] Leave No Trace principles for nature activities
- [ ] Cultural appropriation awareness
- [ ] Fair trade and ethical shopping

## 3. Accessibility & Inclusive Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Wheelchair accessibility of recommended places
- [ ] Accessible hotel room availability
- [ ] Accessible transport options
- [ ] Hearing/visual impairment accommodations
- [ ] Service animal policies by country
- [ ] Accessible bathroom locations
- [ ] Terrain assessment (cobblestones, hills, stairs)
- [ ] Medical equipment availability (oxygen, dialysis)
- [ ] Sensory-friendly experiences (for neurodiverse travelers)
- [ ] Companion/carer discounts

## 4. Family & Multi-Generational Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Age-appropriate activities by child age range
- [ ] Stroller-friendly routes and venues
- [ ] Child car seat regulations and rental
- [ ] Kid-friendly restaurant guide (high chairs, kids menus, play areas)
- [ ] Nap-schedule-friendly itinerary pacing
- [ ] Baby supplies availability (diapers, formula, baby food)
- [ ] Childcare / babysitting services
- [ ] Teen-friendly activities (adventure, independence, social)
- [ ] Elderly-friendly options (pace, accessibility, rest stops)
- [ ] Multi-generational accommodation (connecting rooms, villas)
- [ ] Family bathroom / nursing room locations

## 5. Luxury & Ultra-Luxury Planning

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Private transfers and chauffeur services
- [ ] Concierge-arranged experiences
- [ ] Private tours and guides
- [ ] VIP access and skip-the-line options
- [ ] Private dining experiences
- [ ] Yacht / helicopter / private jet options
- [ ] Personal shopper services
- [ ] Spa and wellness retreats
- [ ] Wine cellar tours and private tastings
- [ ] Michelin multi-star dining reservations (6+ months advance)

## 6. Adventure & Expedition Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Permit requirements (trekking permits, national park entry)
- [ ] Guide requirements (mandatory guide zones)
- [ ] Altitude acclimatization schedules
- [ ] Equipment rental availability and quality
- [ ] Emergency evacuation options and insurance
- [ ] Weather windows and seasonal accessibility
- [ ] Fitness requirements and preparation timeline
- [ ] Water purification needs
- [ ] Communication in remote areas (satellite phone, Garmin InReach)
- [ ] Wildlife safety briefings

## 7. Wellness & Health Tourism

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Medical tourism destinations (dental, cosmetic, elective)
- [ ] Wellness retreats (yoga, meditation, detox, Ayurveda)
- [ ] Hot springs / thermal baths (onsen, hammam, spa towns)
- [ ] Mental health retreats
- [ ] Fitness-focused travel (running tours, CrossFit drop-ins, surfing)
- [ ] Healthy dining guides
- [ ] Sleep optimization (jet lag, hotel blackout, white noise)
- [ ] Recovery after long flights (stretching, hydration, compression)

## 8. Cultural Immersion & Learning

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Language schools and immersion programs
- [ ] Cooking class deep-dives (multi-day vs. single session)
- [ ] Art/craft workshops (pottery, painting, textile)
- [ ] Music lessons or jam sessions
- [ ] Dance classes (tango, salsa, traditional)
- [ ] Farm stays and agricultural tourism
- [ ] Monastery stays / spiritual retreats
- [ ] Homestay options (authentic cultural exchange)
- [ ] Historical reenactments and living history
- [ ] Local sport participation (surfing, martial arts, cricket)

## 9. Event-Based Travel

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Festival calendar for destination (with exact dates)
- [ ] Major events during trip (sports, concerts, exhibitions)
- [ ] Ticket purchasing and availability
- [ ] Event-specific accommodation strategy (prices surge)
- [ ] Transport during events (road closures, extra services)
- [ ] Dress code for events
- [ ] Safety during large gatherings
- [ ] Pre/post event activities

## 10. Shopping & Souvenir Intelligence

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Local specialties worth buying (not tourist junk)
- [ ] Authentic vs. counterfeit guidance
- [ ] Tax-free shopping process
- [ ] Customs allowances for returning home
- [ ] Best markets and their specialties
- [ ] Flagship stores and designer outlets
- [ ] Shipping/mailing purchases home
- [ ] Bargaining guide (where, how much, when to walk away)
- [ ] Antiques and art — export restrictions
- [ ] Food/drink to bring home (customs rules)

## 11. Technology & Connectivity Planning

### Research Checklist
- [ ] eSIM providers ranked by destination
- [ ] WiFi calling setup and alternatives
- [ ] VPN selection for restricted countries
- [ ] Offline app recommendations (Maps, Translate, Currency)
- [ ] Charge/adapter ecosystem
- [ ] Photo backup strategy (cloud sync, portable drive)
- [ ] Travel app stack recommendation (Flighty, PackPoint, XE, etc.)
- [ ] Smart luggage and AirTag tracking
- [ ] Portable WiFi hotspot rental

- [ ] Hotel loyalty program stacking
- [ ] Priority Pass lounge locations
- [ ] Status match opportunities
- [ ] Fuel surcharge avoidance strategies
- [ ] Positioning flights for better connections
- [ ] Hidden city ticketing opportunities (with risks)
- [ ] Award availability sweet spots
- [ ] Companion certificates and upgrade strategies
