# Itinerary Builder — Synthesis Methodology

## Principles

1. **Geography-first:** Group activities by area to minimize transit
2. **Energy management:** Alternate high-energy and rest periods
3. **Pace-appropriate:** Match the traveler's stated pace preference
4. **Flexible:** Build in buffer time — plans change
5. **Balanced:** Mix culture, food, nature, relaxation across the trip
6. **Seasonal-aware:** Check opening hours, sunset times, weather patterns

## Synthesis Rules

### Cross-Referencing Agent Outputs
1. Plot ALL recommended places on a mental map by neighborhood
2. Cluster nearby items into half-day / full-day blocks
3. Assign each day a primary neighborhood/area
4. Thread meals from the Food Agent into natural break points
5. Insert Hidden Gems Agent findings as "discoveries" within area days
6. Place splurge meals strategically (not all on day 1)
7. Build in 1 "free day" per week (no fixed plans, explore spontaneously)

### Pacing Rules
- **Packed schedule:** 3-4 activities per day + meals
- **Balanced:** 2-3 activities per day + meals + buffer
- **Relaxed:** 1-2 activities per day + meals + lots of free time
- **Never:** More than 5 planned activities in a single day
- **Always:** At least 1 hour of unstructured time per half-day
- **First day:** Light schedule (jet lag, settling in)
- **Last day:** Pack and departure logistics only

### Transport Transitions
Between every activity, note:
- Mode of transport
- Duration
- Cost
- Walking distance if applicable
- Any tickets/passes needed
