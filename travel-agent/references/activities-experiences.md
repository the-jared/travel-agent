# Activities & Experiences Research Guide

## Research Protocol

### Sources
1. **GetYourGuide** — Curated tours, activities, skip-the-line tickets
2. **Viator** — Extensive tour catalog with reviews
3. **Klook** — Strong in Asia, transport + activities combos
4. **Airbnb Experiences** — Local-hosted unique experiences
5. **Google "Things to do in [city]"** — Knowledge panel results
6. **Atlas Obscura** — Unusual, off-the-beaten-path experiences
7. **YouTube** — "[city] must do", "[city] things to do [year]"
8. **Reddit** — "r/[destination] what to do", "r/travel [city] recommendations"
9. **Local event calendars** — Search in local language for events during trip dates

### Categories to Research (ALL of these)

#### Cultural & Historical
- [ ] Museums (major + hidden/niche)
- [ ] Historical sites and monuments
- [ ] UNESCO World Heritage sites
- [ ] Temples, churches, mosques (visiting etiquette)
- [ ] Traditional performances (theater, dance, music)
- [ ] Art galleries
- [ ] Architecture walks / notable buildings
- [ ] Local festivals during trip dates

#### Nature & Outdoors
- [ ] National parks and nature reserves
- [ ] Hiking trails (easy / moderate / challenging)
- [ ] Beaches (tourist vs. local, swim safety)
- [ ] Gardens and parks
- [ ] Scenic viewpoints / lookouts
- [ ] Sunrise / sunset spots
- [ ] Wildlife viewing opportunities
- [ ] Water activities (kayaking, snorkeling, surfing, sailing)

#### Food & Drink Experiences
- [ ] Cooking classes
- [ ] Food tours (walking, market, street food)
- [ ] Wine / sake / beer tastings
- [ ] Farm / plantation visits
- [ ] Tea / coffee ceremonies
- [ ] Market tours (morning markets, night markets, fish markets)

#### Unique & Memorable
- [ ] Once-in-a-lifetime experiences unique to this destination
- [ ] Workshops (pottery, calligraphy, weaving, etc.)
- [ ] Volunteer opportunities (ethical, responsible)
- [ ] Sports events happening during trip
- [ ] Local classes (surfing, martial arts, dance)
- [ ] Wellness (onsen, hammam, spa, yoga retreat)

#### Day Trips
- [ ] Top 3-5 day trip options from base city
- [ ] Transport to/from (time, cost, logistics)
- [ ] What to see/do at each
- [ ] Worth a day trip vs. overnight?

#### Nightlife & Entertainment
- [ ] Rooftop bars / cocktail bars
- [ ] Live music venues
- [ ] Night markets
- [ ] Night tours (ghost tours, food tours, pub crawls)
- [ ] Cultural evening shows
- [ ] Club scene (if traveler is interested)

### Output Per Activity

```
### [Activity Name]
- **Category:** [Cultural / Nature / Food / Unique / Day Trip]
- **Duration:** [X hours]
- **Cost:** $X per person ([free / budget / moderate / splurge])
- **Booking:** [Required in advance / Walk-in OK / Seasonal]
- **Best time:** [Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Specific time]
- **Location:** [Address + Google Maps link]
- **Transport:** [How to get there from accommodation area]
- **Rating:** [X.X on Google / TripAdvisor / GetYourGuide]
- **Why do this:** [1-2 sentences on what makes it special]
- **Skip if:** [Conditions where this isn't worth it]
- **Combine with:** [Nearby activities to pair it with]
- **Pro tip:** [Insider knowledge — best entrance, photo spot, etc.]
```

### Time Blocking
Tag each activity with optimal time slot:
- 🌅 Best at sunrise
- ☀️ Best in morning
- 🏙️ Best midday
- 🌇 Best at sunset
- 🌙 Best at night
- 🌧️ Good rainy-day option
- 📅 Day-specific (markets on certain days, closed Mondays, etc.)
