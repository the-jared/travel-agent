# Seasonal Events & Calendar Intelligence

## Research Protocol

For EVERY trip, research what's happening at the destination during travel dates.
This can make or break a trip — a festival can be the highlight, or closures
during a holiday can ruin plans.

---

## Search Queries (Run ALL)

1. "[destination] events [month] [year]"
2. "[destination] festivals [month] [year]"
3. "[destination] public holidays [year]"
4. "[destination] what's on [month] [year]"
5. "[destination] [month] things to do"
6. In local language: "[destination] イベント [month]月 [year]年"
7. "[destination] closures [month] [year]"
8. "[destination] school holidays [year]"
9. "[destination] sporting events [month] [year]"
10. "[destination] exhibitions [month] [year]"

## Global Events That Affect Travel

### Events That ENHANCE a Trip
| Event | When | Where | What Happens |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cherry Blossom | Late Mar-Apr | Japan | Parks bloom, hanami picnics, stunning beauty |
| Holi | Mar | India | Festival of colors, massive celebrations |
| Carnival | Feb-Mar | Rio, Venice, NOLA | Parades, costumes, parties |
| Oktoberfest | Late Sep-Oct | Munich | Beer festival, 6M+ visitors |
| Day of the Dead | Nov 1-2 | Mexico | Altars, celebrations, unique culture |
| Songkran | Apr 13-15 | Thailand | Water festival, massive street parties |
| Lantern Festival | Feb | Taiwan, China | Sky lanterns, stunning visuals |
| Northern Lights | Sep-Mar | Iceland, Norway, Finland | Aurora viewing season |
| Midnight Sun | Jun-Jul | Scandinavia, Iceland | 24h daylight, unique experience |

### Events That COMPLICATE a Trip
| Event | When | Where | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chinese New Year | Jan/Feb | China, SE Asia | Everything closes 1-2 weeks, prices spike |
| Golden Week | Late Apr-May | Japan | Domestic travel surge, everything booked |
| Obon | Aug 13-16 | Japan | Travel chaos, accommodation scarce |
| Ramadan | Varies | Muslim countries | Restaurant hours change, reduced services |
| Eid al-Fitr/Adha | Varies | Muslim countries | Mass travel, everything booked |
| Christmas/NY | Dec 25-Jan 2 | Europe | Many closures, reduced hours |
| August holidays | Aug | France, Italy, Spain | Locals leave cities, some shops close |
| Diwali | Oct/Nov | India | Beautiful but pollution spike in Delhi |
| School holidays | Varies | Everywhere | Prices up 30-50%, crowds at attractions |
